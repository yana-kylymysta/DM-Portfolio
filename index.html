How It Works

THE PROCESS

01
Brief & Discover

We start with a call or detailed brief. I need to understand the story, the audience, and the feeling — not just the specs.

02
Music Direction

I put together a reference reel and emotional map. You approve the direction before I touch a single track.

03
Edit & Design

The actual craft. Cutting, designing, layering. Usually 1–2 rounds of feedback to dial in the emotion.

04
Deliver & Mix

Stems, final mix, and all source files. Delivered on time, ready for any platform. No disappearing after handoff.
